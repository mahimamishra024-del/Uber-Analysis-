#  Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Uber ride bookings data to uncover key business insights related to demand, revenue, cancellations, and customer behavior.

The goal of this analysis is to identify operational inefficiencies and provide data-driven recommendations to improve overall performance.

---

##  Key Objectives
- Analyze overall ride demand and revenue trends  
- Understand cancellation patterns and their causes  
- Evaluate vehicle-wise performance  
- Study payment method distribution  
- Identify key business insights for decision-making  

---

##  Dataset
- Source: Ride booking dataset (CSV format)  
- Contains information such as:
  - Booking date  
  - Ride distance  
  - Vehicle type  
  - Booking value  
  - Payment method  
  - Ride status (Completed / Cancelled)

## 📈 Key Insights

### Overall Performance
- Total Bookings: 150K  
- Total Revenue: ₹51.85M  
- Average Daily Rides: ~410  
- Cancellation Rate: ~25%  

➡️ Strong demand observed, but high cancellations impact efficiency.

---

### Vehicle Performance
- Auto dominates bookings and revenue  
- Go Mini & Go Sedan perform consistently  
- Uber XL shows low demand  

➡️ Mid-tier vehicles drive majority of business.

---

###  Revenue & Distribution
- Most rides fall within 5–20 km range  
- UPI is the most preferred payment method (~45%)  

➡️ Digital payments dominate the ecosystem.

---

###  Cancellation Insights
- High cancellation rate (~38%) is a major issue  
- Customer cancellations: plan changes, wrong details  
- Driver cancellations: long pickup distance  

➡️ Optimization of driver allocation is needed.

---

###  Ratings Analysis
- Customer Rating: ~4.40  
- Driver Rating: ~4.23  

➡️ Service quality remains stable despite operational challenges.
